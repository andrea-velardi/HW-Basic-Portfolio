About Me

I was born in California but moved when I was very young to Philadelphia (Go Eagles!).

When I turned 18, I knew I was ready to be "going going, back back, to Cali Cali" (R.I.P. Biggie Smalls)

I moved away from the east coast to attend California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo, CA. And if you are a big Operah fan, she quoted that city "the happiest city in America".

And she is 100% right!! The city is located in a breathtaking crevice of the green rolling foothills, and backs up right to the most beatufil beaches you have ever laid eyes on.

Cal Poly SLO, for short, is where I earned my B.S. in Business with a concentration in Human Resources and Management.

I stayed in SLO to work full time at a software company Mindbody Inc
